India's AI Research Ecosystem and Intelligence !!

India's strides in AI research are significant:


  • India boasts 386 out of 22,000 PhD-educated researchers worldwide, ranking 10th globally in AI research.
  • Globally ranked 13th, India showcases 44 top-notch presenters at leading AI conferences.
  • AI research is primarily concentrated at prestigious institutes like IITs, IIITs, and IISc.


    • Proposed frameworks for AI research and development:


  • Interdisciplinary Cyber Physical Systems (IM-ICPS) suggests a four-tier framework including ICON, CROSS, CASTLE, and CETIT.
  • A two-tier integrated approach is proposed: COREs and ICTAI, aimed at boosting both core and applied research in AI.
